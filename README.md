## ✦ About

I'm a **B.Tech CSE (AI & ML)** graduate on a dedicated research year, deep-diving into **Deep Learning** and **Natural Language Processing** while preparing for **GATE CSE & GATE DA 2027**. I already qualified both exams this year with an AIR of ~6.5K — now I'm back for a sharper rank and a stronger AI/ML graduate profile.

My core research obsession is **Transformer architectures** — building them from scratch, probing what attention heads actually learn, and pushing translation quality for **mid-resource language pairs** like English–Hindi. I don't just call `.fit()` on pretrained models; I like understanding *why* the math works before I trust the output.
